
Name of QuantLet : 'Stacked Barplots - Panel Series'

Published in : Fruehsignale fuer Aenderungen von Konjunkturindikatoren durch Analysen von Big Data

Description : 'Creates two stacked barplots with legend. 
It is a panel series. The barplots are ordered by year 
and within the plots by amount/frequency. The left 
barplot shows the unemployment rate for Germany and 
its states by amount. The right barplot shows 
the frequency of the search term <Arbeitsamt> for 
Germany and its states. The bars are plotted all 
together with a consistent legend.'

Keywords : 'time-series, panel-analysis, financial,
plot, graphical representation, data visualization'
